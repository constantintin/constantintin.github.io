Recommendations

Dune - Frank Herbert

This book and series is still expanding my mind, even after the 7th reread. Herbert moves through countless interesting concepts and future possibilities while writing an exciting and character driven story. What every sci-fi novel aspires to be.

Siddhartha - Hermann Hesse

For all seekers.

The definitive Hesse novel. An adventure of mind and meaning that will leave you deeply changed. This is a must read if you are at all interested in eastern philosophy. And if you are not, you will be after reading.
